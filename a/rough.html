About Event

“Liberty cannot be preserved without general knowledge amongst people, who have a right, from the frame of their nature to knowledge”. To preserve this liberty Centre for Multi-Faceted Learning(CML) has organized an Introductory Session on Understanding General Awareness with Mr. Rajiv Arora whose a Station Director of All India Radio, Bathinda, in online mode on 30th of July 2021, also started his career as a lawyer at Punjab & Haryana High Court and has done PG Diploma in Journalism & Mass Communication. He has illuminated us on different aspects of life. His talks were inspirational and also his wide experience of life and various fields motivated and filled with enthusiasm. The event was a huge success with participants completely satisfied with the outcome.
